1. What is DNA Funded?

DNA Funded is a proprietary trading firm that evaluates traders through simulated trading challenges. Successful traders can progress to a Funded Stage and become eligible for profit sharing under the Rules & Conditions.


---

2. Are trades placed in the real market?

No. All trading takes place in a simulated live market environment. No real market orders are executed.


---

3. Are the profits and losses real money?

No. All account balances, profits, losses, and payouts are based on simulated trading performance.


---

4. What happens if I break a trading rule?

Breaking any trading rule may result in your account being breached, terminated, or your payout being rejected, depending on the violation.


---

5. Can DNA Funded change its Rules & Conditions?

Yes. DNA Funded may amend its Rules & Conditions. Any material changes will be communicated to participants where applicable.


---

6. Can I stop participating in my challenge?

Yes. You may stop participating at any time in accordance with the General Terms & Conditions.

7. Who cannot participate in DNA Funded?

Residents or citizens of restricted countries listed in the Rules & Conditions are not eligible unless specifically approved by DNA Funded.

TRADING CONDITIONS

33. What drawdown model is used?

1 Phase, 2 Phase & Rapid: Balance-Based Daily Drawdown + Static Maximum Drawdown.

Instant Funding: Trailing Drawdown.

24-Hour Challenge: Trailing Drawdown.



---

34. How is the Daily Drawdown calculated?

The Daily Drawdown is calculated from the previous day's closing balance, not from your current equity.


---

35. What is a Static Maximum Drawdown?

A Static Maximum Drawdown is a fixed loss limit based on your starting account balance. It does not increase as your account grows.


---

36. What is a Trailing Drawdown?

A Trailing Drawdown follows your highest account equity. As your equity reaches new highs, the drawdown level moves upward but never moves back down.


---

37. Which challenge types use a Trailing Drawdown?

Only Instant Funding and the 24-Hour Challenge use a Trailing Drawdown.


---

38. What happens if I exceed my Daily Drawdown?

Your account will be considered breached for violating the trading rules.


---

39. What happens if I exceed my Maximum Drawdown?

Your account will be breached and terminated.


---

40. Is drawdown based on balance or equity?

It depends on the challenge:

Standard challenges use a Balance-Based Daily Drawdown and a Static Maximum Drawdown.

Instant Funding and the 24-Hour Challenge use a Trailing Drawdown based on your highest equity.
